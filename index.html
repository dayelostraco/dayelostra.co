About

Trusted by the DoD, DHA, SSA, and CMS, and by global financial institutions, to architect platforms that move billions in transactions, scale to millions of daily users, and survive the audit afterward. Currently CTO at Accelera Solutions, delivering ATO-ready AI agents for federal missions.
