# SEO Specialist Review — dayelostra.co (2026-07-13)

Executed from docs/prompts/seo-specialist-review.md against the live site and repo source. Persona: senior technical SEO consultant. All findings evidence-cited.

**Verdict.** The foundational hygiene is genuinely good: clean apex canonicals that agree with the sitemap and RSS, a correct 404, a complete and accurate Person JSON-LD, self-hosted fonts with preloads, inlined CSS, per-post OG images, and a #1 result for the branded "Dayel Ostraco" query. But the site is leaving its two stated priorities on the table: the essays carry no Article/BlogPosting structured data at all, so answer engines and the knowledge graph have nothing machine-readable tying these claims to Dayel as author, and the same three essays are published near-verbatim (identical titles, slugs, and opening sentences) on the higher-authority accelerasolutions.com, where they will win the head-to-head for the exact topic and title queries the personal site is trying to own. The single highest-impact, fully-in-your-control gap is the missing article schema on `src/pages/insights/[...slug].astro`.

## Quick wins (single pass, under an hour total)

1. Add trailing slashes to internal links: `Navbar.astro:20`, `index.astro:763`, `[...slug].astro:133 & 147`, and the 4 inline `[...](/insights/...)` links in the three `index.md` files. (C)
2. Add `BlogPosting` JSON-LD to `[...slug].astro`, reusing the `Layout.astro` inline-script pattern, with a nested `author` Person carrying `sameAs`. (A + G)
3. Set `ogTitle={post.data.title}` in the `[...slug].astro` `<Layout>` call. (H)
4. Add the Accelera team-page URL (and any speaking/ORCID profile) to the homepage `sameAs`. (G)
5. Append a keyword clause to each of the three article `<title>` tags, leaving H1s untouched. (E)

## Explicitly considered and rejected

- **Cross-domain rel=canonical between the two sites:** rejected; both self-canonical by deliberate choice. Differentiation offered instead (B).
- **Broadening titles/keywords to commercial or generalist AI topics:** rejected per the deliberate federal-only positioning.
- **Any GTM/third-party schema injector or analytics tag:** rejected under the strict CSP; self-hosted beacon already in place.
- **FAQPage schema:** rejected; Google no longer shows FAQ rich results for most sites, and it fights the essay voice. Definition sentences (F) serve LLM extraction better.
- **Breadcrumb schema, tag/pagination pages, Blog/ItemList graph:** rejected on a nine-page, two-level site.
- **Reworking the homepage bio image for LCP:** inspected; not the LCP element, and the inlined-CSS, font-preloaded build already handles CWV risk.
